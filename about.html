About Us

We don’t just renovate houses.
We help people feel at home again.

Who We Are ｜ 我们是谁

YS House Beautician 是一家位于吉隆坡与雪兰莪的装修与家居美化团队， 专注于全包装修、旧屋翻新与空间提升。 我们帮助屋主打造一个不只是“好看”， 而是真正住得舒服、用得安心的家。

YS House Beautician is a renovation and home beautification team based in Kuala Lumpur and Selangor. We specialise in full renovation, home beautification, and space transformation — creating homes that not only look good, but feel comfortable and last.

Why We Started ｜ 我们为什么开始

我们成立 YS House Beautician， 是因为看到太多屋主在装修过程中承受不必要的压力。 价格不透明、沟通不清楚、工程品质不稳定， 甚至完工后找不到人负责，都是常见的问题。

We started YS House Beautician after seeing too many homeowners suffer from stressful renovation experiences — unclear pricing, poor workmanship, and contractors who disappear after completion.

我们相信，装修不应该是一件让人担心和后悔的事， 而是一个值得期待、安心交付的过程。

We believe renovation should be a transparent, well-managed, and worry-free journey for every homeowner.

How We Work ｜ 我们如何进行每一个项目

每一个项目开始之前，我们都会先了解您的需求、生活方式与预算， 而不是一味推荐昂贵或不必要的设计与材料。 我们注重实用性、耐用度与整体协调感。

Every project starts with understanding your needs, lifestyle, and budget. We do not push unnecessary designs or materials. Instead, we focus on practical solutions, quality workmanship, and details that make a real difference.

从规划、施工到交付，我们全程监督与沟通， 确保每一个细节都到位。

From planning to execution and handover, we manage the process carefully so you can renovate with peace of mind.

Our Promise ｜ 我们的承诺

我们把每一间房子都当成自己的家来对待， 尊重您的空间、时间与信任。 工程完成后，我们依然愿意负责与协助， 因为这不只是一次交易，而是一份责任。

We treat every home as if it were our own. That means respect for your space, attention to detail, and responsibility even after the project is completed.
